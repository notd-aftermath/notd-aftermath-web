Patch summary

  • Changes to Chapter 3: Lost Cause
  • Reworked Ground Illumination Flares
  • Quality of life improvements
  • Full game polish (gold pass) across NotD:AM

Patch details

Gameplay Changes

  • William Pierce, a familiar name from the endgame credits, finally emerges from the depths of the game files. This deadly marksman will join Golf company on Hell mode when there are 5 or more surviving players.
  • Added an XP bonus if players clear Lost Cause with all technicians unharmed.
  • Golf company claymores won't get triggered by technicians or blue Golf marines. Also, they won't spawn near Technicians.
  • Killing friendly minis during Lost Cause can cause Article 15.
  • Players can get ailments when attacked by Zulus during Lost Cause.
  • Hits taken during Lost Cause will count.
  • Ground Illumination Flares are invulnerable, except on Deathmatch and Lost Cause. Reduced their HP to 15. They no longer spawn a separate attackable unit for each player.
  • Flares and Ground Illumination Flares will give vision to all AI marines, including enemy marines during Lost Cause.
  • The Communications Array creates two-way shared vision between the players and the brown and light blue AI players but not with civilians in town or with Golf company.
  • The Communications Array can create shared vision with the brown AI player even before the convoy mission.
  • Squad rating gets recalculated whenever a player loads their code, picks a hero or dies.
  • [Assaultman] Reduced the Javelin's minimum firing range from 1500 to 1000. Now it's no longer bigger than the Assaultman's sight range, which is 1100.
  • [Assaultman] Against flying targets, the Javelin will use direct attack mode instead of top-down attack. In this mode, the missile reaches the target faster.
  • [Combat Engineer] Reduced the mana cost of Insert Shell from 3 to 2.
  • [Flamethrower] Reduced Burst Fire cast range.
  • When a hero dies with an armor item or a Rusty Mining Pick, the item won't disappear. It it's a non-hero, the item will appear immediately instead of after the corpse decays.
  • Increased the healing provided by Freedom from 35 to 60.
  • Nanosteel Net's armor debuff will get applied when the net hits the target, not as soon as the ability is used.
  • Moved one of the airfield watch towers and removed another.
  • Removed a tree near the southwest cave.
  • Killing evil dogs and evil civilians now grants hero experience, just like with all other enemies.
  • Killing Golf company tanks and helicopters now grants more hero experience compared to killing marines (used to be less than that).
  • Reduced the cast range of Optical Scope for mini marksman and sniper from 4000 to 3000 to match the range of the corresponding hero ability.
  • Increased the AoE of Medusa's radiation from 600 to 1000. Now it matches the AoE of Eris' radiation.
  • Increased the chance to get Medusa when more players are alive. Added a chance to get Medusa on Nightmare mode.
  • Tagging (CTRL + left click) a unit that needs medical attention will make nearby brown AI medics try to help. If the unit is your own hero, a yellow exclamation mark will appear over the hero.
  • You can no longer tag neutral units (dogs for example) for needing medical attention.
  • Players will once again get a smaller XP reward if Prometheus is killed after Residence 41 is destroyed and there's at least 1 surviving player.
  • The MQ-3 Predator returns to the random pool but with conditions: the player must have 1 million experience or more and there have to be at least 8 starting players.
  • When an array radar is placed, it will take a few seconds until the Transceiver can be used on it (while the radar is connecting to the network).
  • The Transceiver still deals AOE damage if its target died just before the EM damaged it.
  • Reduced the Shiva's max range from 9000 to 8000.
  • Toggle Safety works during Deathmatch.
  • The Grab Nearest Item ability now works the same way as right clicking the item.
  • Players who leave without picking a hero can no longer increase the number of enemy spawns.
  • The USS Independence will accept corpses for evacuation more often until it starts moving.
  • At the start of the M242 Chain Gun mission, before the first part is dropped, Delta Force will head for the center of the map instead of a random LZ.
  • Added some Hunter spawns to Revelation and the M242 Chain Gun mission.

Visual & Sound Changes

  • Players will be listed with no rank until they load their code or pick a hero (as opposed to being listed as a private).
  • Claymore detonations can cause tinnitus.
  • The daytime transition now always happens exactly at 6 am.
  • Added day/night cycle to Deathmatch.
  • Trees will shake when damaged by MD5 mines.
  • SSgt. Thompson and his team arrive with parachutes.
  • [Forward Observer] The marine now changes his animation slightly while channeling Orbital Artillery.
  • [MQ-3 Predator] Adjusted the angle of the first person mode camera.
  • You can no longer hear Bernard reload his shotgun after he uses it at the end of Intro C.
  • Enabled some sound effects found in the game files: a unique sound effect for the Moirae tracker and a looping sound that plays inside the tunnel.
  • Inside the tunnel, the camera will behave like in FPS mode. Players are recommended to use the arrow keys for navigation.
  • Changed the visual effect of the Leviathan's Fire Breath.
  • The TITAN nuke has a bigger mushroom cloud (in the Containment endings).
  • New -sitrep 5 shows hero energy on the multiboard.
  • Increased the distance between the camera and the Bradley tank during the bunny minigame to allow people to see the bunnies better.
  • During replays, the camera won't automatically pan to where Hades/Diablo pulled a player.

Quality of Life Changes

  • New command: -target. Displays the name of the unit currently targeted by the Transceiver.
  • Removed the -abort command. From now on all you need to do in order to select a different target for the Javelin Missile System is to use the item on the new target.
  • Units can stack some items if their inventory is full. Works with grenade launchers and grenades, shotguns and shotgun shells, explosives and explosives cases, magazines and ammunition cases and Javelin components. Only works if the unit is standing near the item. Dogmeat can't stack items.
  • Force Recon's RAVEN Sentries will be easier to spot.
  • Reduced Roshan's acquisition range from 500 to 100.
  • If there was no red player in the lobby, access to the -kick, -sm and -dmclass commands will be given to the first player present instead.
  • If there was no red or orange player in the lobby, ownership of that player's Forward Station (Tent) will be given to the first player present in that squad instead.
  • The player who selects the difficulty (usually red) will hear a beep when the intro cinematic is over, just like the other players.
  • Players will automatically start reloading their main weapon if they were out of ammo when they got resupplied by the USS Independence.
  • The -report command now shows surviving players before dead players and shows the class of dead players too.
  • The Munitions Count officer ability also displays the ammo in the hero's inventory.
  • [MQ-3 Predator] In addition to its other effect, the Predator's Munitions Count ability also does what the officer Munitions Count ability does.
  • Updated the control pings tag system to take into account the ammo in the player's inventory before announcing that they need ammo.
  • First person mode can now also be toggled by pressing the ~ key.
  • The rank experience that dead players gain from killing enemies now gets updated more frequently.
